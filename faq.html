Frequently asked questions

What can I return?

You may request returns for most items you buy from sellers listed on Amazon.in that are within the return window, except those that are explicitly identified as not returnable on the product detail page and/or Amazon.in Returns policy. To know about the Return window for Amazon Business orders, please visit here Items are returnable if you've received them in a condition that is physically damaged, has missing parts or accessories, defective or different from its description on the product detail page on Amazon.in.
